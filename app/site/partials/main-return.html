Pricing Policies

On our website, and through our social media outlets, you will notice that we don’t post prices. In today’s ever-growing competitive landscape it is important for us to keep our pricing competitive. As a result of these price changes, it has become increasingly tough to keep our posted prices correct. In the end, we decided it was easier to not post prices.

What you can expect is the most competitive prices on sneakers, apparel, accessories and soccer gear in Toronto and the GTA. A visit to any one of our locations will show you just how competitive we are.

  1. All major credit cards accepted (Visa, MasterCard, American Express).
  2. Credit card purchases over $150 require a Photo ID.
  3. We offer refunds within 10 days of purchase. Exchange within 30 days with sales receipt on unworn merchandise.
  4. We no longer accept defective merchandise after 60 days from original purchase date.
  5. All returns require original sales receipt.
  6. Refunds or exchanges can be made at any location.
  7. All hats are final sale with no refunds or exchange
  8. 20% to 60% Off Everyday on most items in store.
