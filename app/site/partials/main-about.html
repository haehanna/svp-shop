About SVP

Serving Toronto and the GTA for over 25 years, SVP Sports has become one of the Greater Toronto Area’s number one sneaker and athletic apparel destination.

SVP: Service. Value. Product.

That’s what we stand for, and it is what you can expect. Whether it’s a great deal on a new pair of sneakers, the latest and greatest the sneaker industry has to offer, or that personal favourite you couldn’t get your hand on years ago… SVP will find a way to fill your sneaker needs.

From our massive warehouse on Steeles, to our location in Alliston, to our premium shop in the heart of downtown Toronto on Queen, or one of our six other locations in the GTA… SVP should be your number one sneaker stop.
